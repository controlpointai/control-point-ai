Mission

Ensure AI strengthens accountable human decisions.

ControlPointAI helps technical teams see where AI-influenced work moves before it creates operational effects.

The work focuses on mapping execution flows, defining delegation, enforcing boundaries, and preserving accountability as AI enters defense, maritime, and safety-critical workflows.

Vision

Make responsibility impossible to blur.

The vision is a future where AI operates within clearly defined human authority, delegation is explicit, boundaries are enforced, and responsibility is never ambiguous.

If AI influences a decision, the organization should know what moved, what changed, and who remains accountable.

Informed Approval Model

From principle to mapped flow.

The method is simple: map where AI-generated work moves, name the delegation, define the human authority boundary, and set the conditions that trigger hold, escalation, or re-authorization.

  • Flow Map where AI inputs, outputs, recommendations, and work instructions move between systems.
  • Boundary Identify where human authority must remain explicit and enforceable.
  • Accountability Decide what changes should force hold, escalation, re-review, or re-authorization before proceeding.
