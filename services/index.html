Typical First Engagement

A focused map of one AI-influenced execution path.

The best starting point is usually one workflow where AI-generated work can affect cost, schedule, tasking, support, operations, or release. ControlPointAI maps the flow, identifies uncontrolled propagation, and marks where authority validation belongs.

  • 1. Trace the data flow Where AI inputs, outputs, recommendations, and work instructions move between systems.
  • 2. Locate the authority gap Where operational effects can propagate before a qualified authority validates the work.
  • 3. Place the Control Point What authority, policy, context, budget, schedule, risk, and approval checks must occur before action.
